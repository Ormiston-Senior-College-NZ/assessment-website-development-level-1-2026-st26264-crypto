Join Club of Indian Diaspora!

The Indian Club is open to all students who are interested in learning about Indian culture, traditions, and festivals. You do not have to be Indian to join, everyone is welcome!

Who Can Join?

  • All students are welcome
  • No prior knowledge needed
  • Anyone interested in Indian culture, food, music, or festivals

Student Leadership Opportunities

Students can take part in leadership roles such as helping organise events, and leading activities.
